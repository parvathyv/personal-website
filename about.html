Early Years

  • Grew up in India.
  • Devoured books of myths and tales, especially the illustrated kind.
  • Storytelling fascinated me, these grand and complex worlds made accessible in a fun and easily digested format.
  • This interest has stayed with me through my life, guiding my education and career.

Professional Life

  • Began as a Graphic Designer at Sikorsky Aircraft.
  • Adapted Business Intelligence Development into my skillset.
  • Learnt the importance of communication, and an iterative and creative aproach to navigate the changing needs of business.
  • An user centric product was at the forefront of the work of the entire team.
  • I realized I was telling stories through my work, simplifying complex worlds, albiet of a different kind than the ones I read about in my childhood.

Winds of Change

  • In 2013, I felt a overwhelming need to move closer to my passion for storytelling & design in my career.
  • Websites seem like the perfect medium and I enrolled in a bootcamp for Full Stack Development at Launch Academy.
  • During the bootcamp, I found myself enjoying the Front End aspects a lot, especially the UI of a website.
  • I became a mother.

Current Path

  • I wanted a deeper understanding of the complete process that leads to creating websites that leave a mark on the user.
  • The UX Immersion course at Careerfoundry gave me a well rounded perspective on the process of UX.
  • I learnt about detail oriented research and analytical methods and also applied a Mobile First approach to my projects.



"From analog to digital, the tools of content creation and distribution have changed tremendously over time, and yet the importance of a human centered approach to storytelling remains the same."
